FAQ: Your MetaTrader and Funded Trading Questions Answered
What's the main difference between MT4 and MT5?
While both platforms offer robust trading capabilities, MT5 supports more asset classes, provides additional timeframes, and features an improved backtesting environment for Expert Advisors.
How do I transition from a demo account to a simulated live funded account?
After successfully completing Vault Funder's evaluation process, you'll be provided with login credentials for your simulated live funded account on MT4/MT5.
Are there any restrictions on trading strategies when using a funded account?
While we encourage innovative trading strategies, all trades must adhere to Vault Funder's risk management policies, which are designed to protect both the trader and the firm.
Can I access my Vault Funder account on mobile devices?
Absolutely. You can download the MT4/MT5 mobile app and log in with your Vault Funder account credentials for on-the-go trading.
